Who We Are

Origin

Born from the dark,
built to endure.

Crimson Moonflare formed in 2024 out of a shared obsession with heaviness, honesty, and the kind of music that doesn't flinch. Four people who found each other through a mutual exhaustion with everything safe and polished.

The band draws from the emotional weight of metalcore and the rawness of alternative rock — blending crushing riffs with melodies that lodge themselves in your chest and refuse to leave. Themes of mental health, identity, and rebellion run through every track.

Independent from day one. No label. No compromise. Just the music, the people who make it, and the ones who need it.

What We Stand For
Authenticity

No filters, no pretense. Every lyric, every riff comes from a real place. The music exists because it has to, not because it's marketable.

Community

Fans aren't an audience — they're the reason any of this works. Mental health, belonging, and being heard are at the core of what we create.

Independence

No label, no compromises. Full ownership of the art means it stays exactly as heavy, honest, and unpolished as it needs to be.
